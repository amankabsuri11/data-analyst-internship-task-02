📊 Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

 Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data using **Power BI** to identify trends, profitability, and business opportunities. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Python (Pandas)
- CSV Dataset

---

## Dataset

- SampleSuperstore.csv
- 9,994 Records
- 13 Columns

---

## Dashboard Features

- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Average Discount KPI
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Profit by Region
- Sales by State
- Profit by State
- Profit by Sub-Category
- Sales by Segment
- Interactive Filters

---

## Key Business Insights

- Technology generates the highest sales and profit.
- Furniture has high sales but relatively low profitability.
- West is the most profitable region.
- California contributes the highest sales.
- Texas records the highest overall loss.
- Tables are the biggest loss-making sub-category.
- Higher discounts generally reduce profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus on Technology products.
- Review Furniture pricing strategy.
- Reduce discounts on low-profit products.
- Investigate losses in Texas.
- Improve profitability of Tables and Bookcases.
